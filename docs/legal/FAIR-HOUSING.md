# Fair Housing (Draft)

> **Draft for portfolio and demonstration purposes only — not legal advice.** Brokerages must comply with federal, state, and local fair housing laws. Consult qualified counsel.

**Contact:** compliance@propai-os.com

---

## 1. Equal Housing Opportunity

PropAI OS supports **Equal Housing Opportunity** principles consistent with the U.S. Fair Housing Act and applicable state laws.

We do not tolerate use of the Service to discriminate against any person because of **race, color, religion, sex (including gender identity and sexual orientation, where applicable), handicap (disability), familial status, or national origin**, or other protected classes under applicable law.

---

## 2. Role of PropAI OS

PropAI OS is a **software tool for licensed brokerages and their agents**. PropAI:

- **Does not** act as a brokerage, landlord, seller, or housing provider
- **Does not** make housing decisions, screen applicants, or approve transactions
- **Does not** determine whether a listing or communication complies with fair housing law — **brokerages and agents remain responsible**

Customers must ensure listings, advertisements, AI-generated descriptions, search filters, and communications comply with fair housing requirements.

---

## 3. Customer responsibilities

Brokerages and users agree to:

- Use accurate, non-discriminatory language in listings and marketing
- Avoid preferences or limitations based on protected characteristics (e.g., “no children,” “perfect for Christians,” “English speakers only”)
- Apply CRM and pipeline practices without unlawful steering or exclusion
- Train agents on fair housing obligations and review AI-suggested copy before publish
- Respond promptly to discrimination complaints per internal policies and law

Violations may result in suspension or termination under our [Terms of Service](./TERMS-OF-SERVICE.md).

---

## 4. Prohibited uses on the platform

Examples of prohibited use include:

- Publishing or promoting discriminatory listing content
- Configuring filters or workflows designed to exclude protected classes
- Using lead scoring or AI to unlawfully rank or reject prospects based on protected characteristics
- Harassment or hate speech in notes, messages, or public forms

Report suspected abuse to compliance@propai-os.com.
